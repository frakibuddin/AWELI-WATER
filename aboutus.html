ABOUT US

THE ECO-INITIATIVE

Aweli Water was founded in 2022 by Jennifer, a lawyer who was passionate about promoting sustainability and reducing plastic waste

Sustainable Sourcing & Packaging Excellence in Sweden

✔ Our target market comprises health-conscious individuals seeking a reusable alternative to single-use plastic water bottles, particularly those who appreciate flavored water.

✔ Our spring water is sourced from two high-quality lakes in the Telemark region of Norway: Lake Farris and Lake Mjøvann. The Telemark Basin is an award-winning water source, consistently ranked among the best drinking water sources in Europe.

✔ Our cartons are crafted from 88% renewable materials

✔ We are headquartered in Sweden and are steadfast in our commitment to using sustainable, recycled materials for our packaging.
